ESA LAB

The laboratory adheres to the spirit of innovation and practicality, and cultivates talents with practical ability, innovative design and integration of software and hardware, and actively enhances students' vision and professionalism.

The laboratory is committed to embedded systems and other related technologies, and provides students with relevant experiments and cross-session cooperation.

Research Direction

  • Embedded Systems
  • Wearable System
  • Biomedical Signal Processing
  • Design of Portable Biomedical Electronic System

Development Characteristics

  • Attach importance to cooperation with the industry to match theory with practice.
  • Actively encourage students to participate in various professional competitions, and won many awards, which is highly recognized.
